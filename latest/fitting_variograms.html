Fitting variograms

Fitting variograms

Fitting theoretical variograms to empirical observations is an important preprocessing step to ensure valid mathematical models of spatial continuity for posterior estimation/simulation with variogram-based methods such as Kriging and sequential Gaussian simulation.

Given an empirical variogram, the fit function can be used to perform the fit:

fit

Currently the following fitting methods are implemented:

Weighted least squares
